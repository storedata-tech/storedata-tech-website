About
Purpose & scope of StoreData IP Ltd

StoreData IP Ltd was incorporated to centralise the ownership and governance of software assets developed for and used by the StoreData Group. Its mandate is to ensure that core components are maintained, documented and made available to group companies in a consistent and controlled way.

The company does not advertise, market or sell products or services to third parties. All activities are strictly intra-group and support the operational entities that interact with end customers.

  • Centralised ownership of key software modules and technology assets.
  • Clear segregation between operational business and IP management.
  • Transparent, documented intra-group licensing model.

Activities
Core activities & income sources

StoreData IP Ltd focuses on a clearly defined set of activities related to software and intellectual property:

  • Development & maintenance of reusable software modules and technology components.
  • Documentation & governance of the group’s intellectual property, including technical specifications and usage guidelines.
  • Internal licensing of these components to affiliated companies within the StoreData Group under documented intercompany agreements.

The company does not engage in consulting, hosting, support services or any form of trading with external entities.

Income profile
The majority of income consists of internal licence fees paid by group companies for the use of software modules and related technology.
Exclusions

Group
Position within the StoreData Group

StoreData IP Ltd is part of the StoreData Group and operates as the central intellectual property entity. It is owned by the group holding company, which in turn holds the operating subsidiaries that interface with customers and markets.

This structure ensures that software components and technology assets are managed with clear legal ownership and consistent technical standards, while leaving commercial and client-facing activities to the dedicated operating companies.
